Welcome to the TempelChat Help Center! Here you’ll find answers to common questions and tips to get the most out of your TempelChat experience.

1. Getting Started

How do I create an account?

  • Click on the Sign Up button on the homepage.
  • Enter your details and verify your email.
  • You’re ready to start chatting!

How do I log in?

  • Click the Log In button.
  • Enter your username and password.
  • If you forgot your password, click Forgot Password to reset it.
